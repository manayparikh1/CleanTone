What it does

A full cleanup chain in one click

Every file runs through the same studio-style processing pipeline that audio engineers reach for — automatically tuned for instruments and voice.

🔇

Noise gate & reduction

A smart downward expander measures your noise floor and silences hiss, hum, and room tone in the gaps between notes — without chopping the music.

🪨

Rumble removal

A high-pass filter clears out low-end rumble, AC hum, and handling thumps that muddy the mix and waste headroom.

✨

Clarity & air

Targeted EQ lifts the presence and "air" bands so the instrument sits up front, crisp and detailed instead of dull and distant.

🎚️

Even dynamics

Gentle compression smooths out the loud and quiet parts so nothing disappears and nothing jumps out.

📈

Loudness normalize

A final normalization pass brings the whole track up to a strong, consistent level — loud and full, but never clipping.

🔒

Totally private

Everything happens locally in your browser using the Web Audio API. Your recordings never get uploaded to any server.

How it works

Three steps. Seconds, not hours.

No accounts, no installs, no audio engineering degree required.

1

Drop your file

Add any MP3 or WAV recording — flute, violin, voice, guitar, anything. It loads instantly and shows you the original waveform.

2

Enhance it

Pick a preset or fine-tune the sliders, then hit Enhance. CleanTone runs the full cleanup chain in seconds.

3

Compare & download

A/B the original against the enhanced version, watch the noise vanish from the waveform, then download your clean WAV.
