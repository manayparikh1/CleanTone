What it does

Everything your recording needs, automatically

The same processing chain audio engineers use, built right into your browser.

🔇

Noise reduction

Removes background hiss and room noise without hurting the sound of your instrument.

🎚️

Rumble removal

Cuts low-frequency handling noise and AC hum that makes recordings sound muddy.

✨

Clarity boost

Lifts the presence and high-end sparkle so your instrument sounds crisp, not dull.

📈

Volume normalization

Brings the whole track up to a loud, consistent level without clipping anything.

🎵

Warmth control

Adjust the low-end body — more warmth for strings, less for voice or wind.

🔒

Totally private

Everything runs in your browser with the Web Audio API. Your file never leaves your device.

How it works

Three steps and you're done

No sign-up, no installs, no waiting.

1

Upload your file

Drop in any MP3 or WAV — guitar, flute, voice, anything. It stays on your device the whole time.

2

Pick a preset and enhance

Choose a preset for your instrument type, tweak the sliders if you want, then hit Enhance.

3

Compare and download

Listen to both versions side by side, then download the cleaned-up WAV when you're happy with it.
